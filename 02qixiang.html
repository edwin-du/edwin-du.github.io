Smart Meteorology System

智汇气象系统

Mar 2021

智汇气象系统以3D可视化表现综合气象统计信息、实时气象变化、气候预警等，打造气象状态分析决策，以沉浸式交互行为模拟真实空间进行可视化展示，从宏观到微观，以更直观、易理解和易操纵的方式呈现给决策者。


Smart Meteorology System provides 3D visualization of comprehensive meteorological statistics, real-time meteorological changes, climate warnings, etc., to analyze and make decisions about meteorological conditions. It simulates real space with immersive interactive behavior and presents to decision-makers from macro to micro in a more intuitive, understandable and controllable way.
