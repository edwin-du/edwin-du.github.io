Smart Shanghai Transportation System

上海智能交通引擎

Oct 2019

智汇上海交通系统集地铁、公交、换乘于一体，以3D可视化表现线路运营、能耗、客流量、早晚高峰出行压力，打造一体化出行分析决策，对城市交通承载力监控、预测和管控，提升交通系统运行效率和管理水平。以沉浸式交互行为模拟真实空间进行可视化展示，从宏观到微观，以更直观、易理解和易操纵的方式呈现给决策者。


Smart Shanghai Transportation System integrates subway, bus and transfer, and provides 3D visualization of line operation, energy consumption, passenger flow, and morning and evening peak travel pressure, creating integrated travel analysis and decision-making, monitoring, forecasting and controlling urban traffic carrying capacity, and improving transportation system operation efficiency and management level. Immersive interactive behavior simulates real space for visual display, from macro to micro, and presents to decision makers in a more intuitive, easy to understand and easy to manipulate way.
